# SIDIAN CONSORTIUM [Industrial Union 09]

"We Dig. You Profit."

Welcome to the heavy-extraction hub of the Sidian Consortium. This portal serves the miners, mechanics, and logistics masters of the Shard-Yard.

## [ CONSORTIUM OVERVIEW ]
The Sidian Consortium is the galaxy's premier industrial salvage and heavy-mining union. Based in the hollowed-out moon of the Shard-Yard, we specialize in stripping ruined dreadnoughts and extracting raw ore from the most dangerous regions of the Fracture.

## [ INDUSTRIAL TOOLS ]
- **Grit-Walker Maintenance:** Interactive dashboard for monitoring and repairing hydraulic actuators, reactors, and scanners.
- **Scrap Inventory:** Real-time tracking of raw Kybian stock and industrial metals.
- **Operations Board:** Overview of current veil-mining, logistics, and refinery status.
- **Recruitment Panel:** Contract opportunities for those willing to join the line.
